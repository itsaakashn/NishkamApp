## 5. Accuracy of Sanskrit and Translations

We have made every reasonable effort to ensure the accuracy and integrity of:
- Sanskrit verse text
- Transliteration (Roman script rendering)
- English translations and meanings

However, the Bhagavad Gita is a complex Sanskrit text with centuries of scholarly interpretation. Minor variations in translation exist across different traditions and editions. We acknowledge:
- Our translations are interpretive, not strictly literal.
- Sanskrit is a highly precise language; some nuances may be lost or simplified in English.
- Verse numbering may vary slightly across different editions of the Gita.

If you find an error or inaccuracy, please report it to us at **theaighth@gmail.com** — we take content accuracy seriously.
